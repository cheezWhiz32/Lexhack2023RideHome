About Us

How many times have you, a student, stayed late at school because you found out your bus
had been cancelled 5 minutes before school ended? Oddly specific, but probably more
times than you can count. Such is the condition in my town; there is so much miscommmunication between
bus companies and schools that children have to deal with missed buses, hour long delays, and
worst of all, rides home with the principal regularly. Ride Home is a platform intended to solve
this communication gap and make life easier for students. We connect schools and bus companies on a
common platform so that rather than sending tedious emails back and forth, companies and schools can
inform each other about updates a lot more efficiently.
