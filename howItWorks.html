For Companies

As we explained in our about us page, this website/app is basically a platform for you to connect with the schools you provide transportation to.
And if you're wondering, this will help students as well as you. You can say goodbye to the tedious back-and-forth emails. This platform will your company have a direct link with the schools you are working with.

 

For Students

Confused how this will help you? You're in the right place. Ride Home will make your
life easier by providing your school and bus company a clear source of communication. This will prevent any mishaps or
accidents like last second bus cancellations or hour long delays. Once you get Ride Home,
you'll be able to monitor your bus's status in real time and find out about any updates beforehand.
